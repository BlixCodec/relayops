| ------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

| ✅ Multiple roles                                            | The app supports Dispatcher and Operations Manager contexts.                                                                      | Roles are selected from the role-select screen.                                         |
